Work About me Resume

I love food, going to concerts, and playing Tetris.
My name is Catherine Jade de Leon, but please...

Call me Cat.

I think I've always been a designer without ever having said it aloud. I was "building" websites in elementary school on Neopets PetPages and it continued on the middle school and high school when I was restyling my Xanga blog and my MySpace page.

I've aged myself pretty extensively here, haven't I?

Don't get me wrong, "building websites" is in quotes for a reason. The earlier stages were just pasting in GIFs, links, and maybe text marquees if I really wanted to be fancy. Later, it was copying, pasting, and tweaking style sheets to my liking.

It might have been this familiarity with web language that helped me in college with my introductory computer science courses. While I enjoyed these frustrating courses, I pursued math instead, because at the time, I thought math was the only thing I was equally good at and interested in. By the end of my five years at SJSU, I learned I was very wrong but I was already a year overdue and just wanted to get out of school.

After graduating, I was sitting on my expensive diploma and a lack of passion. It would be that way for an additional five years, while working jobs that let me do the only thing I liked about math - solving problems - and doing it the only way I know how; by trying to make people's lives easier.

I wish there was a poetic moment to how I got here, to how I realized everything I've done in the past has culminated to my decision of wanting to pursue design, but the reality is this: my brother, a UX designer, showed me what he does.

Why aren't I already doing this?

So here I am.

If you happen to catch me off my laptop, I might be playing tennis, watching a basketball game, singing karaoke, taking pictures with my phone, or befriending your pet. :)
